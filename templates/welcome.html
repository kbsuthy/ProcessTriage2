Welcome to Process Triage

Think of this as a checkup, not a full exam. This app helps you quickly assess a process to understand its frequency, friction, risk, and improvement potential—without getting lost in the details. By classifying processes first, we can focus time and effort where it will make the biggest difference.

When automation potential is identified, you can choose to go deeper with AI to better understand pain points and explore where automation could help most.
